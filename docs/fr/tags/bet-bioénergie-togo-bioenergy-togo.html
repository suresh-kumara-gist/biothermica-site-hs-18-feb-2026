BioEnergy Togo

BioEnergy Togo is an NGO soon incorporated whose goal is to carry out a project of collection and valorization of residual materials and biogas-solar co-energy in the great Kloto. 

For more information, please visit (in French) Bio Énergie Togo (bioenergietogo.org)
