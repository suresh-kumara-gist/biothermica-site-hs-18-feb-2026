Projects

Since 1990, the  BIOTOX® regenerative thermal oxidizer has been used successfully in more than twenty-five (25) installations in Canada and the U.S. Biothermica’s  BIOTOX® RTO projects have demonstrated destruction efficiencies of more than 99% and energy efficiencies greater than 90%, for flows varying from 2,000 ACFM to more than 150,000 ACFM. Here is a selection of several projects:
