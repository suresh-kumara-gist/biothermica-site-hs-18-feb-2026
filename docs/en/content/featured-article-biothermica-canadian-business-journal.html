Featured article on Biothermica in the Canadian Business Journal

Saturday, June 1, 2013

Published in the June edition of the Canadian Business Journal, this article features Biothermica's accomplishments since 1987 in the fields of air pollution control, landfill gas services, and carbon and energy project development. The following projects are showcased: Biothermica's 25 MW landfill gas Power Plant in Montreal, the Nejapa landfill gas CDM project in El Salvador and the ventilation air methane (VAM) carbon project in Alabama, based on the company's proprietary VAMOX® technology.
