Raphaël Bruneau, Director-Carbon Markets, is a featured speaker at the 2013 Methane Expo, in Vancouver, Canada

Thursday, March 14, 2013

The 2013 Methane Expo took place March 12 -15, 2013 in Vancouver, Canada. It was organized as a collaboration between the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency and the government of Canada. Conference proceedings are available here (Biothermica presentaton below).

Mr. Raphael Bruneau, Director-Carbon Markets, provided an update on the company's ventilation air methane (VAM) destruction activities and the projects under development with Walter Energy in Alabama, USA.
