BioEnergy Haiti

BioEnergy Haïti is a non-for-profit organization established in Haitian law, dedicated to the implementation of an integrated waste collection, disposal and utilization system, including the landfill site and landfill gas power plant, in Gonaïves, Haïti. This initiative is the result of a collaboration between Terre des jeunes, Biothermica, and several civil society groups located in the  Gonaïves area.
