VAMOX®

Biothermica develops, finances, builds and operates carbon and energy projects from Ventilation Air Methane (VAM) released by underground coal mines.

 

As an integrated developer, Biothermica manages all the aspects of its carbon and energy projects:

  • Feasibility studies
  • Development
  • Financing
  • Permits and authorizations
  • Implementation (engineering, procurement, construction & commissioning)
  • Operation and monitoring
  • Monetization
