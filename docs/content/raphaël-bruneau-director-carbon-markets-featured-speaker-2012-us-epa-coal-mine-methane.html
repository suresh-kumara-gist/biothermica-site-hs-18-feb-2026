Raphaël Bruneau, Director - Carbon Markets, is a featured speaker at the 2012 US EPA Coal Mine Methane Conference, in Las Vegas, USA

Monday, September 24, 2012

The 2012 US CMM Conference took place September 24, 2012 in Las Vegas, USA. It was organized by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. Conference proceedings are available here (Biothermica presentaton below).

Mr. Raphael Bruneau, Director-Carbon Markets, provided an update on the company's ventilation air methane (VAM) destruction activities and the projects under development with Walter Energy in Alabama, USA.
