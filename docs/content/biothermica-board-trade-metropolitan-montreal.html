Biothermica - Board of Trade of Metropolitan Montreal

Tuesday, December 1, 2015

A strategic forum on natural resources, projects, issues and business opportunities organized by the Board of Trade of Metropolitan Montreal will take place Monday December 7, 2015 at the Palais des congrès de Montréal.

Mr. Guy Drouin, President of Biothermica is a featured speaker and his conference titled "Decarbonization of the Quebec economy - the place for the renewable energy"
